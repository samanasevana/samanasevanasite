---

- **"Venerable Jeffrey"** (common address)
- **"Venerable Jeffrey Hancuff"** (written formal name)
- **"Bhante"** (Pali term for a monk, traditional address)
- **"Bhante Guṇavīro"** (formal direct address)
- **"Tan Guṇavīro"** (Thai respectful address)
- **"Jeff" or "Jeffrey"** (birth name, least commonly used)
